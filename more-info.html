Essential Information for Your Taniti Adventure:

Here are a list of facts to help you plan and have the best visit possible:

  • Power Outlets: Standard 120 volts, compatible with U.S. devices—no adapters needed!
  • Alcohol Regulations: Sales and service stop from midnight to 9 a.m. Drinking age is 18, with relaxed enforcement.
  • Language: English is widely spoken, especially by younger Tanitians; older rural residents may use the local dialect.
  • Healthcare: Rest easy with one hospital and several clinics staffed by multilingual professionals.
  • Safety: Violent crime is rare, but petty crimes like pickpocketing are on the rise with tourism—stay mindful and enjoy worry-free.
  • Holidays: Numerous national holidays may adjust attraction and restaurant hours—check ahead to maximize your visit.
  • Currency: U.S. dollars are king, though euros and yen are accepted. Exchange currency at banks, and use major credit cards almost everywhere.
Equip yourself with these insights for a smooth, enjoyable stay on our beautiful island.

Your dream vacation starts here—let Taniti captivate your heart and soul.

Getting to and exploring Taniti is a breeze with a variety of transportation options tailored to your needs.


Arriving on Taniti:

  • By Air: Fly into our small, efficient airport, currently welcoming small jets and propeller planes.
    Exciting expansions are underway to accommodate larger jets soon!
  • By Sea: Set sail on a weekly cruise ship that docks at Yellow Leaf Bay for a magical one-night stay.

Exploring The Island

  • Public Buses: Hop on reliable buses running in Taniti City from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. daily.
  • Private Buses: Venture beyond the city with convenient private services.
  • Taxis: Easily available in Taniti City for quick trips.
  • Rental Cars: Pick up a car near the airport for flexible exploration.
  • Biking: Rent bikes (helmets mandatory) from various vendors and pedal through the island’s scenic routes.
  • Walking: Enjoy the flat, walkable terrain of Taniti City and Merriton Landing on foot.
Plan your journey with ease and embrace the freedom to explore Taniti at your own pace.
